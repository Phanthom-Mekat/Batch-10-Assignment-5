Important Faq

How can I donate to the Flood Relief in Noakhali?

You can contribute directly by entering the amount
you wish to donate and clicking the "Donate" button.

Where will my donation for Feni's flood relief be used?

Your donation will be used to provide emergency aid, including food, water,
and medical supplies, to those affected by the floods in Feni.

How can my donation help the injured from the Quota Movement?

Your contribution will support medical treatments, rehabilitation, and essential
care for individuals injured during the Quota Movement.

Is there a minimum donation amount?

No, every donation, big or small, is appreciated
and makes a significant difference in relief efforts.
